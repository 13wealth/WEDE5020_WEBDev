Our Daily Journey

A full day of care, learning, play, and growth — step by step from morning to afternoon.

Drop-Off
07:00 – 08:30

Parents hand over their little bees with a smile. Our teachers welcome each child warmly, ensuring they feel comfortable and excited. Morning greetings, bag check-ins, and settling into the classroom start the day on a positive note.

Learning & Play
08:30 – 11:30

Children engage in structured lessons, storytelling, art, music, and group play. Activities are designed to build creativity, problem-solving, and social skills while keeping learning fun and interactive.

Meals & Rest
11:30 – 13:30

Healthy, balanced meals are served to refuel active little minds. After mealtime, children enjoy a quiet nap or relaxation period. This helps them recharge and get ready for more afternoon fun.

Pick-Up
14:00 – 17:00

Parents return to collect their children, who leave with stories, new skills, and smiles. Teachers provide a short update on each child’s day, ensuring parents stay connected with their learning journey.
