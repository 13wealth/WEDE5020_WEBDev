CLASSROOMS

Our classrooms are designed to provide a conducive learning environment, equipped with modern technology and resources. Entering our classroom, you will immediately notice a calm but busy atmosphere, where the children are involved in small groups or individual activities. The children are given freedom within a structured environment to choose activities that interest them, and to work with the materials for as long as they need. We support the need for each child to develop their learning and their own individual pace.
